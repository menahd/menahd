# Public interventions, publications and media - Menahd Ouchenir

This page gathers public talks, interviews, articles, podcasts, panels and contributions by Menahd Ouchenir on practical uses of data, AI and digital transformation in public-sector, territorial and economic contexts.

The selection covers smart cities and smart territories, digital government, AI sovereignty, cybersecurity, telecommunications, public procurement, FinTech, logistics, urban mobility and the institutional conditions that make technology useful at scale.

## Main themes

- Artificial intelligence, data strategy and digital sovereignty
- Smart cities, cognitive cities and territorial digital platforms
- Public-sector innovation, govtech and civic technology
- Cybersecurity, critical infrastructure and digital resilience
- Telecommunications, 4G/5G, logistics and connected infrastructure
- Public procurement, pre-sales strategy and technology adoption
- FinTech, SME financing and data-driven economic development
- Urban mobility, sustainability and public-policy performance

## Why this page exists

- To document public contributions where technical subjects are connected to institutional, economic and operational realities.
- To make recurring work visible across AI, data, public-sector transformation, smart territories and digital infrastructure.
- To keep a chronological index of public references that can be cited, shared or reviewed.
