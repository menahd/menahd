# Public Interventions, Publications and Media - Menahd Ouchenir

A selection of public interventions, interviews, articles, talks and contributions around smart cities, digital government, public-sector innovation, urban mobility, civic-tech, technology policy and data-driven decision-making.

## Featured Topics

- Smart cities and smart territories
- Public-sector digital transformation
- Digital government and civic-tech
- Urban mobility and sustainable development
- Cloud, data, AI and technology adoption
- Public-policy performance and operational efficiency

## Selected Highlights

- Speaker and panelist on digital technology, sustainability and public-sector innovation.
- Contributor to white papers and institutional publications on smart cities, human cities and urban mobility.
- Interviewed by public-sector and technology media on cloud adoption, digital transformation and civic technology.
- Author and contributor on public-policy performance, smart nations and digital services for local authorities.
