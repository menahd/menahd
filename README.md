# Hey, I'm Menahd

**Data and AI builder** focused on practical tools for decision-making, public-sector innovation, and operational intelligence.

I build data and AI tools for people who need to understand complex situations, make decisions under constraints, and turn messy information into usable systems.

My work sits between engineering, strategy and public-interest technology: RAG prototypes, decision dashboards, pricing analysis, operational intelligence, smart territories, digital sovereignty, cybersecurity and public-sector transformation.

I care about one thing in particular: making technology useful beyond the demo. Useful for decision-makers. Useful for operations. Useful for institutions, teams and people who have to act in the real world.

## What I build

- **AI-powered applications**: RAG prototypes, document question answering, AI assistants, semantic search and business-facing copilots.
- **Data products**: dashboards, decision cockpits, pricing analysis tools, forecasting workflows and operational analytics.
- **Machine learning workflows**: pricing intelligence, feature importance, regression models, Bayesian analysis, predictive maintenance and benchmarking.
- **Public-sector and smart territory tools**: digital services, civic-tech prototypes, urban operations analytics and citizen-facing interfaces.
- **Strategic prototypes**: lightweight products that connect technical feasibility, user adoption and institutional constraints.

## What drives me

I like turning unclear problems into tools people can use.

The work usually starts with questions like:

- what decision are we trying to improve?
- what information is scattered, underused or hard to understand?
- what should be automated, explained or made searchable?
- what would make this useful for both technical teams and decision-makers?

That is the space I enjoy most: building practical systems while keeping the strategic, institutional and human context in view.
